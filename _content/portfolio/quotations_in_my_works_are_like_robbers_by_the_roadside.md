## Quotations In My Works Are Like Robbers By The Roadside

_Quotations In My Works Are Like Robbers By The Roadside_

Lucas Battich

2015

This web-based work is inspired by internet culture production of image-macros with quotations, and specially by the practice of "troll quotes," which misattribute all three elements of the image (photograph, quotation and author).

This work generates a new quotation image-macro by random association among elements upon the action of the user, by using the arrow keys or simply with a mouse-click.
