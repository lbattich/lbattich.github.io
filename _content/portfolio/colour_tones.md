## Colour Tones

_Colour Tones_ is an online generative film work and sound composition, where a colour is chosen at random from the whole RGB range. The sound tones are generated depending on the hue and luminosity of the random colour. The work produces a unique colour-sound composition everytime.

(This work uses the more-or-less-new WebAudio API, which may not function on old browsers.)

Algorithm-based work, 2014.
