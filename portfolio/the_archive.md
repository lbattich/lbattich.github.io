## The Archive

Filmed at various locations currently in use for the EYE Collection, _The Archive_ presents a mosaic incursion into the vast physical infrastructure, its thousands of items and dedicated professional care, that lies at the heart of the EYE Film Institute. The film portrays the archive in a unique transitional state, as all its contents are being relocated to a new purpose-built Collection centre in Amsterdam Noord.

This film was made in collaboration with Solange Frankort and Thomas Walskaar.
